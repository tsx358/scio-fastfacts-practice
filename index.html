SciO Fast Facts Practice Site

Fast Facts is an Event of Inquiry and Nature of Science.
About the competition:
Each competition has 3 rounds, which each begins with the supervisor giving each team a scoresheet that contains a grid with 5 different science categories listed along the horizontal axis and 5 different letters listed along the vertical axis. Categories and letters are not repeated within a grid or among the 3 grids used in competition. Teams have 6 minutes to complete each round.

Click "Generate" to generate a new random table!
