SciO Fast Facts Practice Site

Fast Facts (SciO Event) is an 2022 official Event of Inquiry and Nature of Science Events.
About the competition: Each competition has 3 rounds, which each begins with the supervisor giving each team a scoresheet that contains a grid which has 5 different science categories listed along the horizontal axis and 5 different letters listed along the vertical axis. Categories and letters are not repeated within a grid or among the three grids used in competition. Teams have 6 minutes to complete each round.

For this practice site, due to my coding issues, there may be more than 1 category/letter in the grid (Please contact me if you can identify the "error" in my code!). Click "Generate" to generate a new random table (You don't type anything..., try to see if you can fill the table out).
